KCMC SMS system

2016 ||| Skills: SMS API development ||| URL: Project website

The research institute at the KCMC hospital wanted to develop an SMS system to remind HIV infected pregnant women to take their medicine. This system required that the SMS be sent at irregular times not draw suspicion it's automated. The system also had to take input in natural languages. I succesfully created it and the project won funds,
